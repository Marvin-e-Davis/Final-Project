The Dark Patterns Quiz

What are Dark Patterns?

  1. Fearsome forms of deception
  2. Tricks built to decieve users
  3. Interfaces designed to benefit the developers

How Do Dark Patterns Affect You?

Dark Patterns may cause you to...

  1. Accidentally make an extra purchase
  2. Be unable to easily find options (deleting your account, unsubscribing, etc)
  3. Share more information than intended
  4. Lose sight of your original goal due to built in distractions
  5. Feel guilty for opting out of a service

How to Avoid Dark Patterns.

  1. If it seems to good to be true don't click on it.
  2. If you wouldn't tell a stranger personal information don't tell the internet
  3. If you're subscribing or paying for something always confirm you're paying the intended amount
  4. If you want to stop subscribing to something always make sure to know how to do so before hand, and check to confirm you've cancelled the subscription

Why Should You Take The Dark Patterns Quiz?

The Dark Patterns Quiz is an effective assessment for identifying and correcting internet habits that make you more succeptible to Dark Patterns.

  1. Do you make accounts for random websites and give away personal information?



  2. Do you click on advertisements that promise free benefits with a download?



  3. Do you check your shopping cart before confirming a purchase?



  4. Do you read what you click on websites if you think you know the layout of the site?



  5. Do you sign up for subscriptions and forget if you're still paying for them?
