Dark Patterns

What are Dark Patterns?

Dark Patterns are aspects of websites and applications intentionally designed to decieve, trick, and take advantage of users. Whether to steal your data, your money, or damage your devices, Dark Patterns act as a way to covertly control their user base. Many companies' websites', especially ones that sell products, employ the usage of Dark Patterns.

How Do Dark Patterns Affect You?

Have you ever explored a website that at closer inspection seems shady? Have you ever been asked randomly to give away personal information, told told to click links for prizes, or struggled with completing a task on the website due to it's lingo. If you answered yes to any of these questions you might have encountered Dark Patterns.

How to Avoid Dark Patterns

Do you make accounts for random websites and give away personal information?



Do you click on advertisements that promise free benefits with a download?



Do you check your shopping cart before confirming a purchase?



Do you read what you click on websites if you think you know the layout of the site?



Do you sign up for subscriptions and forget if you're still paying for them?
